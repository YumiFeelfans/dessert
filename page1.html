Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a dessert made from cocoa powder or real chocolate. It has a rich, sweet flavor and a soft, moist texture. It is often topped with chocolate cream or chocolate chips and is one of the most popular desserts in cafés and bakeries worldwide.
